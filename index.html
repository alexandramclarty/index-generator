Quick Links Script Generator

Generate browser console scripts to add video chapter indexes to CETL SharePoint pages

How to use this tool
1. Fill in the page filename, video URL, and paste your chapter timestamps below.
2. Click Generate Script and review the parsed chapters.
3. Click Copy Script to Clipboard.
4. Go to any page on the CETL SharePoint site in your browser.
5. Press F12 to open Developer Tools, click the Console tab.
6. Paste the script and press Enter.
7. Wait for the SUCCESS message, then refresh the target page to verify.
8. The page is saved as a draft — review and publish when ready.
